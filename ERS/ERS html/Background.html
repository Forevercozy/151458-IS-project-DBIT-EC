EMPLOYEE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

BACKGROUND

Good Business management is one of the major backbones of effectively running a business , gaining profitability and helping to improve economic stability as a whole. One of the ways to promote good business management is having an easy way to store, update and record employee data . This will help managers reduce time wastage by keeping records in an analogue manner while also helping them focus on more pressing issues. Local managers do not have a way to keep track of their employee count thus making them lose on a lot of good business aspects due to lack of proper management. Due to this the entire economy as a whole loses out. Businesses do not gain growth meaning no economic growth thus no Job opportunities. In order for us to solve this, I believe helping managers better manage their business in terms of their Employee management can increase business growth and down the line , country - wide economic growth. Even though this will prove quite helpful , we might still face some challenges as this is the nature of things; most local managers do not have access to IT based machinery such as laptops and computers and also might not have enough funds to purchase systems of small or large magnitudes. To help on this, I recommend considering the pricing factor and reducing the price of the system quite much just for the sake. My proposed system, the Employee Record System , will assist local managers in business management and hopefully with them achieving great management , our whole economy will benefit.
